Research Interests

My research seeks to explain how strategic leaders interpret and respond to diverse stakeholder pressures. I draw upon social movement theory and upper echelons theory to explore how protests, employee activism and shareholder unrest shape corporate governance and stakeholder strategies.

  • Stakeholder strategies: How firms manage pressures from multiple stakeholders and balance economic and social goals.
  • Corporate governance: The role of boards and executives in responding to social movements and activist investors.
