Research Interests

My research focuses on how organizations innovate and compete in rapidly changing environments. I explore topics such as technology strategy, platform ecosystems, and the economics of data-driven decision making.

Current Projects

  • Digital Platforms and Market Design: Investigating how platform architecture and governance influence innovation and competition among complementors.
  • AI and Strategic Decision Making: Examining the impact of artificial intelligence on managerial decision processes and firm performance.
  • Entrepreneurship and Ecosystems: Studying how entrepreneurial ventures leverage ecosystem relationships to scale and create value.

Working Papers
