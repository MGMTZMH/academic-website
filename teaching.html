Teaching Philosophy

I aim to create an engaging and interactive learning environment that encourages students to link theory with practice and critically examine the role of business in society. My teaching integrates case studies, experiential exercises and discussions about stakeholder perspectives to develop strategic thinking and ethical leadership.
